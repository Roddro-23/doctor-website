Meet the Doctor

Dr. Sarah Johnson

MBBS, MD (Cardiology), FCPS

Dr. Sarah Johnson is a board-certified cardiologist and general physician who has dedicated over 15 years to providing exceptional healthcare. She completed her medical education at Dhaka Medical College and her Cardiology fellowship at the National Institute of Cardiovascular Diseases (NICVD).

Dr. Johnson believes in treating each patient as a whole person, not just a diagnosis. She combines the latest medical advancements with a warm, approachable manner to create a comfortable environment for all her patients.
